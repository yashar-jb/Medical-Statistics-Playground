Medical Statistics Playground EBM Arcade · Levels 1–8

Pick a playground (level) to explore a specific concept in medical statistics and evidence-based medicine. Each one is a self-contained HTML file: ideal for live demos, teaching sessions, or just playing with ideas.
Select a level · Launch a lab

How it works No install · Offline · Pure HTML

  • Click a card to open that playground (EBMPG#.html) in your browser.
  • Change sliders / numbers and watch **graphs & statistics update in real time**.
  • Use them as live demos while teaching EBM, or as an interactive study tool.
Tip: On GitHub Pages, this index.html is your entry screen. For a talk, keep your slides + this page open, then alt-tab between them.
